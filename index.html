Welcome To Lakshmi Construction

We are the best Interior designer since 2018.

We offer comprehensive design packages that include project management from start to finish. This helps remove the stress and worry of remodeling or redecorating while still involving you in the process.

Layering patterns and textures with a mix of custom and found vintage objects, and pulling from a global design perspective, each space reflects the client's own personality.

OUR WORK

We design curated residential spaces that are a beautiful reflection of what's most important to you. Our portfolio spans all style disciplines, from traditional to contemporary and everything in between.

No two assignments are the same

My role is to interpret my clients wishes, resolving the pragmatics of form and function, to create a seamless integrated whole. Highly functional, serene and beautiful, resonant and refined, a place to luxuriate in the moment by moment experience of life.
